1. About the App

TelsizApp is a modern educational assistant developed for candidates preparing for Amateur Radio License exams (Class A, B, and C exams organized by KEGM in Turkey). The app allows users to access up-to-date question banks and track their progress.

2. Purpose and Target Audience

  • Purpose: To simplify the amateur radio licensing process through a user-friendly interface and smart study algorithms.
  • Target Audience: Exam candidates aiming to obtain an amateur radio license and enthusiasts looking to refresh their knowledge.

3. Privacy and Data Protection (Zero-Data Policy)

TelsizApp prioritizes user privacy:

  • Local Data Processing: All progress and statistics are stored entirely in your device's local memory (Core Data).
  • No Personal Data Collection: No personal data such as name, email, or location is collected or transmitted to remote servers.
  • Third-Party Sharing: Your data is never shared with third-party organizations or advertising networks.
